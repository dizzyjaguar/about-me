This is a fun quiz about Jazz!

It all started somewhere

The first recorded Jazz song was February 26th, 1917. Although some people argue that jazz was concieved in 1895

It certainly had a noticeable stylistic evolution

  1. Ragtime was created sometime in the 1890's
  2. Swing! Also known as the 'Big Band Era' came about around 1935. Known as dance music, it's impossible not to tap your foot when you listen
  3. Bebop came around after the Big Band Era and players started playing in smaller groups like trios, quartets, and quintets. It was mainly based on improvisitaion
  4. And a few more after, but aren't as important

Some of Jazz's Most Renouned Names were known for a Reason!

  • Errol Garner was known for his full and extravagant chordal sound
  • Charlie Parker is known as the "God Father" of Bebop. He changed the improvisation game dramatically.
  • Thelonius Monk is well respected as being 'himself' really playing his original sound with stabbing dissonant chords and blues drag vibe
  • Miles Davis is one the most known names in Jazz, he played with Charlie Parker in his early days and just like everyone else who played with Parker, paved his own path and made a big impact on the Jazz world
